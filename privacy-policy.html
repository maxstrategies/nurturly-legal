Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Summary: Your baby's data stays on your device and your iCloud. We never see, collect, or store your personal tracking data. We collect only anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

1. Who We Are

Nurturly ("we", "our", "us") is a baby tracking application developed by Marko Maksimovic, based in Serbia. Nurturly was born from a personal need — as a new father, I wanted a simple, beautiful way to track my baby's daily routine and development. What started as a tool for my own family grew into an app designed to help all parents.

This policy explains how we handle your information when you use Nurturly (the "App").

2. Data That Stays On Your Device

All of your baby's tracking data is stored locally on your device and, if you choose to enable it, synced to your personal iCloud account. This includes:

  • Sleep, feeding, diaper, and health logs
  • Photos and journal entries
  • Milestones, reminders, and doctor visit records
  • Baby's name, date of birth, and gender
  • Memory book content

We never upload, access, or store this data on our servers. When iCloud sync is enabled, Apple's CloudKit handles the synchronization under Apple's own privacy policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.

3. Photos

Photos you add to journal entries or the baby book are stored exclusively on your device and in your iCloud account (if enabled). Photos are never uploaded to any server we operate. If you disable iCloud sync, photos exist only on your device and may be lost if you delete the app.

4. Subscriptions

Nurturly offers premium features through in-app subscriptions. Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store and managed via RevenueCat, a subscription management platform. When you subscribe:

  • Your purchase is handled entirely by Apple — we never see your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID password.
  • RevenueCat receives an anonymous subscriber identifier and your subscription status (active, expired, etc.) to manage entitlements. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any baby tracking data.
  • You do not need to create a separate account to use Nurturly or its premium features. Your Apple ID handles authentication.

5. Anonymous Analytics We Collect

To understand how the app is used and to improve it, we collect anonymous, non-personal analytics. This data cannot be used to identify you or your baby. We use the following services:

PostHog (product analytics) — Tracks which features are used (e.g., "someone logged a sleep entry"), app opens, and onboarding completion rates. No personal data is sent. PostHog assigns a random anonymous identifier that is not linked to your identity.

Sentry (crash monitoring) — If the app crashes, Sentry receives a technical report containing the device type, operating system version, and the code location where the crash occurred. No personal data, baby names, or tracking content is included in crash reports.

What we never collect: Your baby's name, date of birth, health data, sleep/feeding/diaper details, photos, notes, journal entries, or any content you enter into the app.

6. Third-Party Services

The services listed above process only anonymous data as described. Here are their privacy policies:

  • Apple (App Store & iCloud) — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — Privacy Policy (subscription management)
  • PostHog, Inc. — Privacy Policy (product analytics)
  • Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) — Privacy Policy (crash monitoring)

We do not use any advertising networks, and we do not share any data with advertisers.

7. No Personal Account Required

Nurturly does not require you to create a username, password, or provide an email address. Premium features are unlocked through your Apple ID via the App Store — no separate sign-up is needed. The app works fully offline for all core tracking features.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Nurturly is designed for parents and caregivers, not for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The baby data you enter into the app remains on your device and iCloud — it is not transmitted to us.

9. Data Retention

Your tracking data exists only on your device and iCloud. If you delete the app, locally stored data is permanently removed. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months and then automatically deleted. Subscription status data is managed by RevenueCat and Apple according to their respective retention policies.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. However, if you have concerns about the anonymous analytics data, you can contact us and we will assist you. You can also:

  • Disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's Settings
  • Delete all local data by deleting the app
  • Manage iCloud data through your Apple ID settings
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings

11. International Users

Anonymous analytics data may be processed in the United States by our third-party service providers (PostHog, Sentry, RevenueCat). This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
